2017 ACM Richard Tapia Celebration of Diversity in Computing Conference

Published Thu, Sep 28, 2017 by Erica Ram

This past weekend, the 2017 Richard Tapia Celebration of Diversity in Computing Conference took place in Atlanta, GA at the Hyatt Regency Atlanta.

My Microsoft DS3 2016 teammate Louise Y. Lai and I presented our poster on our project Airbrb: Predicting Loyalty. The poster session was held Thursday night for two hours, and for those two hours we had a continuous flow of interested people asking us about our project, much to our delight.

At the closing banquet, we were awarded 3rd place in the poster competition.
